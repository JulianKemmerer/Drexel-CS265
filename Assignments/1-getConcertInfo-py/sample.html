How to Submit Concert Information to the XPN Online Calendar
To submit Concert & Event information, email your request to the Online Staff. If this email link does not work, our email address is "Online@xpn.org." Be sure to include the Date of the Event, Artist, Venue, and the hyperlink/URL of the venue. You will need to include this info within the email, not as an attachment, due to the ongoing threat of viruses attached to email documents.
